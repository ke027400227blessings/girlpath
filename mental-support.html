🧠 Quick Coping Tools

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Identify 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

Sensory Reset

Hold an ice cube, splash cold water on your face, or squeeze a stress ball.

Safe Person

Text one trusted person right now. You don't have to explain, just say "Hi".

Change Environment

Step outside for 2 minutes or move to a different room.
