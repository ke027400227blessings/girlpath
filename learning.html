📚 Learn Smarter, Not Harder

💡 Pro Study Tips

Evidence-based techniques to help you remember more in less time.

🔄 Active Recall

Don't just re-read. Close your book and test yourself. Asking questions forces your brain to retrieve information.

⏳ Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 1 week). This fights the "forgetting curve".

🍅 Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Keeps your brain fresh and prevents burnout.

🎨 Mind Mapping

Draw connections between ideas visually. Great for essays, history, and complex science topics.

😴 Sleep & Study

Your brain consolidates memory while you sleep. 8 hours of sleep is better than 2 hours of cramming.

🤝 Teach It

Explain the concept to a friend (or your pet!). If you can teach it, you truly understand it.

📝 Exam Preparation Checklist

Check off these tasks to ensure you're fully ready before exam day.

Create a revision timetable (2 weeks before)
Gather all past papers and mark schemes
Pack your bag: Pens, pencils, calculator, ID
Check exam location and transport route
Sleep 8 hours the night before
Eat a healthy breakfast on exam day
Arrive 15 minutes early
